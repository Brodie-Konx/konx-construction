# SEO Audit Report
## Konx Construction Group

**Audit Date:** April 3, 2026
**Website:** https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com
**Industry:** Commercial Construction (Tilt Panel Repair, Commercial Fitout, Concrete Works)
**Target Market:** Perth, Western Australia

---

## Executive Summary

Konx Construction Group has a **strong foundation** with good on-page SEO fundamentals and clear value propositions. However, there are **critical technical SEO gaps** (missing robots.txt and sitemap.xml), limited **content depth** compared to major competitors, and **keyword targeting opportunities** that are being underutilized.

**Key Findings:**
- Good foundational on-page SEO with proper title tags and meta descriptions
- Critical technical SEO issues: No robots.txt or sitemap.xml files
- Competitors have significantly deeper content and higher domain authority
- Opportunity to establish thought leadership through tilt panel repair and concrete works content
- Missing structured data (schema.org markup) for LocalBusiness, Service, and FAQs

**Estimated Potential:** With targeted improvements, Konx can rank for high-intent Perth construction keywords within 6-12 months.

## 3. Technical SEO Analysis

### Critical Issues (MUST FIX)

#### Issue 1: Missing robots.txt
**Severity:** HIGH
**Finding:** robots.txt returns 404
**Impact:** Search engines may not crawl efficiently; inconsistent indexing

**Recommendation:**
Create `/public/robots.txt`:
```
User-agent: *
Allow: /
Disallow: /api
Disallow: /admin
Crawl-delay: 1

Sitemap: https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/sitemap.xml
```

#### Issue 2: Missing sitemap.xml
**Severity:** HIGH
**Finding:** sitemap.xml returns 404
**Impact:** Google cannot efficiently discover all pages; crawl efficiency reduced

**Recommendation:**
Create sitemap for all 4 pages:
```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-04-03</lastmod>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>1.0</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/services</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-04-03</lastmod>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>0.9</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/about</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-04-03</lastmod>
    <changefreq>quarterly</changefreq>
    <priority>0.8</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/contact</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-04-03</lastmod>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>0.8</priority>
  </url>
</urlset>
```

#### Issue 3: Missing Structured Data
**Severity:** HIGH
**Finding:** No LocalBusiness, Schema.org, or FAQSchema markup detected
**Impact:** Missing rich snippets in search results; Google doesn't understand business details

**Recommendation:**
Add to `<head>` on all pages:
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Konx Construction Group",
  "image": "https://www.konxconstructiongroup.com/logo.png",
  "description": "Perth-based commercial construction company specializing in tilt panel repair, commercial fitout, and concrete works.",
  "telephone": "0478530664",
  "email": "info@konxconstructiongroup.com",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "addressLocality": "Perth",
    "addressRegion": "WA",
    "postalCode": "6000",
    "addressCountry": "AU"
  },
  "areaServed": {
    "@type": "City",
    "name": "Perth"
  },
  "priceRange": "$$",
  "hasOfferCatalog": {
    "@type": "OfferCatalog",
    "name": "Construction Services",
    "itemListElement": [
      {"@type": "Offer", "name": "Tilt Panel Repair"},
      {"@type": "Offer", "name": "Commercial Fitout"},
      {"@type": "Offer", "name": "Concrete Works"}
    ]
  }
}
```
